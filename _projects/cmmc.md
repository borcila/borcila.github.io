## Context
Small manufacturing startup with no dedicated IT staff and an extremely limited budget aiming to become CMMC 1 compliant to set its foundation toward becoming CMMC 2 compliant in the future

## My Role
- Sole individual responsible for cybersecurity documentation and assessment
- Worked directly with business owner to align controls to real-world operations

## Work Performed
- Conducted full CMMC Level 1 self-assessment using CNCEdge
- Mapped controls to NIST 800-171
- Identified gaps and compensating controls
- Built an evidence index to support compliance claims

## Deliverables
- Completed self-assessment
- Evidence index
- Written cybersecurity policies

## Constraints & Decisions
- Prioritized low-cost, high-impact controls
- Focused on documentation clarity for future audits
- Balanced security with business usability
